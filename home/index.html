WHO WE ARE
What We Believe

    Great design makes people happy; happy people do great things! The stunning optimization of form and functionality lies somewhere between science and art. When you sit on the chair, sleep on the bed, look on the artwork, you will feel the balance. Enjoy it!

    Homes and spaces will evolve with you; let them tell your story, bear your scars, celebrate your memories; not look like another model flat with cheap imports

    Sustainability is not a buzzword; we keep experimenting to make products and find solutions that don't destroy the planet. Plantation/recycled timber, solar, rain water harvesting, vertical gardens. You will see us trying elements of these with all our design.
